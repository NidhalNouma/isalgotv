What to Include in Your Request

Strategy Description

A clear explanation of how your strategy works — the indicators, conditions, or logic it relies on.

Entry & Exit Rules

Define when to open a trade and when to close it. The more specific, the better — include conditions for both long and short positions if applicable.

Screenshots

Include chart screenshots showing correct entry and exit points. Visual examples help us understand your strategy faster and build it accurately.

Additional Details (optional)

Any extra context such as preferred timeframes, risk management rules, stop-loss/take-profit levels, or asset types.

Things to Know

Private & Source-Protected

If your strategy idea uses one or more features from our published strategies, it will be built as a private-only strategy. The source code will not be shared it remains fully protected.

Future Changes Are Quoted Separately

Any modifications or new features you'd like added to your strategy after delivery will require a separate quote. Each update is scoped and priced independently.

Earn From Your Strategy

If your strategy meets our quality standards, it may be eligible to be published as an Exclusive strategy on IsAlgo allowing you to earn from other users who subscribe to it.
